Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use timzinin.com and associated services, we may collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and social media profile data when you authorize third-party platform integrations (e.g., TikTok, YouTube, Instagram).
  • Usage data: Analytics data about how you interact with our services, collected via Umami (privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics).
  • Content data: Text, images, and videos you submit for publishing through our content distribution tools.
  • OAuth tokens: Access tokens for authorized social media platforms, stored encrypted and used solely for content publishing on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide consulting and content publishing services you requested
  • To publish content on social media platforms you have authorized
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you about your account or services

3. Third-Party Platform Access

When you authorize our application to access your social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.), we:

  • Only request the minimum scopes necessary for content publishing
  • Store access tokens securely with encryption at rest
  • Never share your tokens or account data with third parties
  • Allow you to revoke access at any time through the respective platform's settings

4. TikTok Integration

For TikTok Content Posting API integration specifically:

  • We request video.publish, video.upload, and user.info.basic scopes
  • Videos are published directly to your TikTok account with your explicit approval
  • AI-generated content is labeled with the AIGC flag per TikTok's requirements
  • You can revoke access at: TikTok App → Settings → Manage App Permissions

5. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted (AES-256) on secure servers
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Content data is processed and not retained after publishing unless required for analytics
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke platform access at any time
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

8. Cookies

We use privacy-focused analytics (Umami) that does not use cookies or track users across websites. No advertising cookies are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact
